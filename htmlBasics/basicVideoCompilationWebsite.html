New Movies Coming Out This Summer

This summer there are many new blockbuster movies coming out. Movies such as Mission Impossible, Superman and The Fantastic Four are premiering during May and July. Lots of great movies to see in theaters this summer.

Mission Impossible - The Final Reckoning

Tom Cruise returns as Ethan Hunt in this action-packed finale, which debuts in the U.S. on May 23, 2025, and features his signature death-defying stunts and an action-packed story.

Superman

James Gunn’s Superman, starring David Corenswet as an established Clark Kent, brings a brighter, humor‑infused tone to the DC universe. Debuts in the U.S. on July 11th.
